Refund FAQ

Answers about App Store purchases & billing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a refund?
Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find your purchase in the list, choose Request a refund from the dropdown menu, and submit.
Can the developer approve my refund?
No. Because transactions are handled securely by Apple, only Apple can approve or process refunds. Developers do not have access to your billing details or the ability to issue refunds directly.
How long is Apple's review time?
Refund decisions typically take 1–5 business days. Apple will notify you about the status of your request via email or on the Report a Problem status page.
What information should I include?
To increase the chance of approval, include the purchase date, the specific item name, a valid reason for the refund (e.g., "accidental purchase"), and any specific error details.
Can you speed up the review process?
The refund review speed is controlled entirely by Apple's internal systems. Developers cannot expedite this process.
